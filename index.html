Beliefs

OSLC Arcadia

Our beliefs are firmly grounded in the doctrine of Grace, Faith, and Scripture. We believe that all are sinful, and it is only through the Grace of God that we are saved. This Grace is given through Faith in Jesus Christ, God’s son. Finally, that the Bible is the Holy infallible inspiration of God.

Choir/Bells

Meets Thursdays at 6pm (Bells) and 7pm (Choir)

The use of choirs in the Church is a tradition that dates back to the very beginning of Christianity. Here at Our Savior Lutheran, we are proud to keep that wonderful tradition alive in honoring and praising God. The "Celebration Choir" is a choral group that plays an important role in the Sunday morning worship experience, and it actively participates in both the 8 am and 10:45 am worship services. The repertoire of the choir includes a wide variety of sacred musical styles, from Gregorian chant, through traditional/classical repertoire, to contemporary and folk selections.  The choir performs three Sundays a month, as well as at special church holidays and occasions. Choir rehearsals are held on Thursdays, 7 pm - 8:30 pm The "Carillon" Hand Bell Choir plays in worship one Sunday a month, and at special church holidays and occasions. Many of our hand bell players are members of our choral group as well. The repertoire of the hand bell choir includes both arrangements of sacred classical and contemporary music. Bell rehearsals are held on Thursdays, 6pm - 7pm. On many occasions throughout the year, we invite musicians from our congregation and from the community to enrich our worship with their talents. During the summer months, Sunday morning worship is musically supported by our vocal and instrumental soloists. For more information or if you are interested in joining, please come to one of our rehearsals or contact our Music Director, Marija.

L.W.M.L

The Lutheran Women's Missionary League is for all women young and old.  As Christian women serving the Lord, it is our goal and prayer to help spread the Good News to all corners of the world.  At meetings, we strive to plan events to help each woman grow spiritually in her own walk as well as use her God-given talents and gifts to reach others.  L.W.M.L. is for women who are devoted to the mission of the church: to carry out the great commission. We have monthly board meetings, typically the first Tuesday of the month.  At these meetings we plan and coordinate the various sponsored events.  You are welcome to join us at 7:00 p.m. in the Martin Luther Classroom.  Watch the bulletin for any changes to the meeting dates. LADIES: As a member of Our Savior Lutheran Church, you are already a member of this special organization.
Mite Sunday is always on the 2nd Sunday of the month.  It is held outside after both the 8:00 a.m. and the 10:45 a.m. church services.  The “mites” you give are put to a variety of projects and they all add up to make a tremendous difference in lives!  Mite Sunday is sponsored by the LWML.
