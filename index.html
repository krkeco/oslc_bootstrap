Who We Are

Our Savior Lutheran Church (OSLC) is part of the Lutheran Church Missouri Synod (LCMS).
We worship in Arcadia, California.

SUNDAY 8:00AM and 10:45AM - Main Sanctuary (Childcare provided)

Traditional Lutheran style worship with Organ, Choir, Hand bells, and Liturgy. Communion is celebrated on the 1st and 3rd Sundays of the month.

Youth and Adult Bible Classes are held in-between services at 9:30AM. SUNDAY  Praise Service -  6:30 PM - Main Sanctuary

Praise worship with Guitar, Keyboard, and Singing. Communion is celebrated on the 1st and 3rd Sundays of the month.

MONDAY Night Service - 6:30PM - Main Sanctuary

Abbreviated (30 minutes) Traditional worship with piano and organ. Communion is celebrated on the 1st and 3rd Mondays of the month.
